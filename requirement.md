Detailed Requirements:

Data Requirements: Sales history, promotional calendar, holiday dates, web search trends,inventory levels, competitor pricing, macroeconomic data

Model Requirements: Multi-horizon forecasting, handling of static and dynamic features,interpretability, probabilistic outputs

Performance Targets: 15-20% reduction in WMAPE (Weighted Mean Absolute Percentage
Error) vs. baseline

Technical Stack: Python, PyTorch, pytorch-forecasting, Temporal Fusion Transformer (TFT),SHAP, FastAPI, Docker

Monitoring: Forecast accuracy dashboards, data drift detection, automated retraining triggers


Industry Use Cases:
- Retail & FMCG – SKU-level demand forecasting (Walmart, P&G, Unilever)
- Pharmaceuticals – Cold-chain inventory and regional demand forecasting (Pfizer, Moderna)
- Manufacturing – Raw material requirements planning (Tesla, Boeing)
- Logistics – Warehouse staffing and route optimization (FedEx, DHL)
- E-commerce – Fulfillment center inventory allocation (Amazon, Shopify)
